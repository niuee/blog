# Banana — Stations & Buildings

A railway network is not complete without stations for passengers and buildings that bring the world to life.

## Station Placement

Stations are placed directly on track segments. The placement system snaps to valid positions along the track.

## Multi-Platform Support

Stations can have multiple platforms, each assigned to a specific track. This allows for complex station layouts with passing loops and terminal configurations.

## Platform-Track Assignment

Each platform is mapped to a track segment, defining where trains stop and which direction they face.

## Station Navigation

The station list panel lets you browse all stations and quickly pan the camera to any station on the map.

## Buildings

Buildings can be placed on the map to fill out the landscape:

- Configurable size presets
- Elevation-aware positioning that accounts for terrain height
