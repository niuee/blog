# Banana — Timetables & Scheduling

Timetables bring order to your railway network, transforming freeform train movement into a coordinated schedule.

## Clock System

Banana has an in-game clock that starts at Monday 6:00. The simulation time drives all scheduled events and train departures.

## Timetable Management

The timetable panel lets you define schedules for each train:

- Departure times from each station
- Dwell times at platforms
- Route assignments

## Simulation Time

The simulation runs in real-time but can be adjusted. The time system coordinates with train movement to ensure trains arrive and depart according to their schedules.
