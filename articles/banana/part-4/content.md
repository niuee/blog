# Banana — Trains & Formations

Trains in Banana use a hierarchical formation system that models real-world train composition.

## Hierarchical Formations

Formations are built from the ground up:

- **Cars** — individual vehicles with configurable properties
- **Formations** — groups of cars coupled together
- **Nested formations** — formations can be combined into larger consists

## Bogies

Each car sits on bogies (wheel sets). The formation editor lets you visually configure bogie placement and spacing.

## Car Stock & Depot

The depot panel manages your fleet of available cars. From here you can:

- View all car stock
- Assign cars to formations
- Create new formations

## Formation Editor

A visual editor lets you build and modify train formations, adjusting car order, bogie configuration, and spacing between vehicles.
